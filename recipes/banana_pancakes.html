Banana Pancakes

Description:

A recipe for how to make banana pancakes from scratch that are ready in minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • An Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas

Steps:

  1. Mix - Combine dry ingredients in one bowl and wet ingredients in a another. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated.

  2. Cook - Pour the batter in 1/4 cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat. Cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula, and cook for another few minutes (or until each side is golden brown).

  3. Serve - Serve your banana pancakes immediately. They're delicious alone or with your favorite pancake toppings.
